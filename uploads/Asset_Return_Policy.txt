=========================================
COMPANY ASSET RETURN & COMPLIANCE POLICY
=========================================

All employees assigned company assets (laptops, mobile devices, accessories, etc.) must adhere to the following rules:

1. Return Schedule:
   Assets must be returned on or before the "expected return date" stated in your allocation receipt.

2. Extension Requests:
   To extend an allocation, request authorization from your Manager at least 48 hours prior to the return date.

3. Failure to Return:
   Unresolved overdue allocations will lead to system access suspension and management escalation.

If you have any questions, contact the IT Admin department.
